First Week of November

Jalen Ngonda "If You Don't Want My Love" September 8, 2023 USA Soul, R&B 10 "If You Don't Want My Love," allows listeners to have the nostalgia of old school soul and R&B brought on by this modern take from Jalen Ngonda.
Kyle Davis "Get Wid Lemonade" February 15, 2024 USA Hip-Hop 8 "Get Wild Lemonade" is a remix of "Lemonade" by infamous rapper "Tay-K." Artist Kyle Davis brings in jazz undertones and brings the song to a new life using the original artists vocals.
Leon Thomas "MUTT" September 27, 2024 USA R&B, Soul, Jazz 9 "MUTT" is one of the songs on Leon Thomas' new album which showcases his talent and influence of jazz and soul through vocals.
Laila! "Not My Problem" June 14, 2024 USA Pop, Hip-hop 7 This song has a rating of 7 because although it is extremely catchy, it can get quite repetitive. But this song about confidence is always a fun listen.
Clairo "Add Up My Love" July 24, 2024 USA Indie Pop 8 "Add Up My Love," has been one of my favorite songs lately as it would fit greatly on the soundtrack of a feel-good Indie movie.
Billie Eilish "CHIHIRO" May 17, 2024 USA Pop 8 Billie Eilish always has a fun version of pop and it shows in her song "CHIHIRO."
Raye "Worth It." February 3, 2023 USA R&B, Pop 8.5 "Worth It." has a jazz entrance and then has a up-beat rhythm with harmonized vocals and playful guitar granting this song an 8.5 on my rating scale.
Amy Winehouse "Stronger Than Me" October 20, 2003 UK jazz, soul 10 This Amy Winehouse starts with a chill drumbeat followed by Winehouse's soft and velvety jazz tone. This song captures confidence and feminism through its lyrics and instrumentals of electric guitar, keyboard, drums, and muted trumpet.
George Benson "Give Me the Night" July 9, 1980 USA jazz-funk, R&B 10 This song is a perfect way to end the playlist as it has a fun up-beat of jazz-funk infused with R&B notes. Makes you want to get up and dance, ready to start the day!
